# Privacy Policy for DC8980 Shipping App

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

## Overview

DC8980 Shipping ("the App") is a warehouse management application designed for Walmart Distribution Center 8980 operations. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

- Door schedule entries and timestamps
- Pallet counting data
- User settings and preferences
- Export history and file metadata

### Voice Data

- Voice commands are processed locally on your device
- No voice recordings are stored or transmitted
- Voice recognition uses your device's built-in capabilities

### No Personal Information

- The app does not collect personal identifying information
- No user accounts or registration required
- No email addresses, names, or contact information collected

## How We Use Information

### Local Data Usage

- Door schedules: Track and manage shipping operations
- Pallet counts: Maintain accurate inventory records
- Settings: Personalize app behavior and preferences
- Export data: Generate reports for operational analysis

### Voice Processing

- Voice commands are processed in real-time for hands-free operation
- No voice data is stored, transmitted, or analyzed
- Voice recognition occurs entirely on your device

## Data Sharing and Transmission

### No Data Sharing

- We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data
- All information remains on your local device
- No third-party analytics or tracking services

### Export Functionality

- Users can manually export data in CSV or ZIP formats
- Exported files are saved to the device's local storage
- Users have full control over where exported data is shared

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only

- All app data is stored locally using device storage
- Data is not synchronized to cloud services
- Information is accessible only through the app interface

### Security Measures

- App uses secure local storage mechanisms
- Data is protected by your device's security features
- No network transmission of sensitive operational data

## Device Permissions

### Microphone Permission

- **Purpose:** Voice command functionality
- **Usage:** Process spoken commands for hands-free operation
- **Storage:** No audio recordings are stored

### Storage Permission

- **Purpose:** Export functionality and app data storage
- **Usage:** Save exported reports and maintain app data
- **Access:** Limited to app-specific directories

### Network Permission

- **Purpose:** Future sync capabilities (currently unused)
- **Usage:** Potential future feature for data synchronization
- **Current Status:** App functions completely offline

## Children's Privacy

The app is designed for workplace use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## Data Retention

### Automatic Retention

- App data persists until manually deleted by the user
- Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
- No automatic data expiration or cleanup

### User Control

- Users can clear all data through app settings
- Individual entries can be deleted as needed
- Export functions allow data backup before deletion

## Your Rights

### Data Access

- View all stored data through the app interface
- Export data in standard formats (CSV, ZIP)
- Complete transparency of stored information

### Data Control

- Delete individual entries or all app data
- Modify settings and preferences
- Control export and sharing of information

### App Removal

- Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
- No residual data remains on external servers
- Complete data removal guaranteed

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through app updates.

## Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Google Play Store privacy requirements
- Apple App Store privacy guidelines

## Technical Information

### Data Types

- **Operational Data:** Door schedules, pallet counts, timestamps
- **Configuration Data:** User preferences and settings
- **Temporary Data:** Voice command processing (not stored)

### No Analytics

- No usage analytics or user behavior tracking
- No crash reporting with personal information
- No advertising or marketing data collection
