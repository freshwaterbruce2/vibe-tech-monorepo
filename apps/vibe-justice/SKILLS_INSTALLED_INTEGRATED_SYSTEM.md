A complete, interconnected system of 4 skills that work together to manage your entire case against Walmart. Think of it as your legal command center.

## The System

### 1. Legal Deadline Tracker

**What it does:** Calculates and monitors all deadlines
**Protects you from:** Missing critical filing windows

### 2. Document Contradiction Detector  

**What it does:** Finds conflicting information across documents
**Proves:** Constructive discharge and bad faith

### 3. Policy Violation Detector

**What it does:** Matches their actions against their own policies
**Proves:** Federal law violations (ADA, FMLA, ERISA)

### 4. Evidence Organizer & Timeline Builder

**What it does:** Organizes everything chronologically
**Creates:** The "story of your case" for attorneys/courts

---

## How They Work Together

```
YOU UPLOAD DOCUMENT
        ↓
Evidence Organizer extracts: date, source, content
        ↓
        ├→ Deadline Tracker: checks for any deadlines mentioned
        ├→ Contradiction Detector: compares with existing docs
        └→ Policy Violation Detector: checks actions vs policy
        ↓
ALL FOUR UPDATE TOGETHER
        ↓
YOU GET COMPLETE ANALYSIS
```

### Real Example: You Upload Sedgwick Letter

**Evidence Organizer:**

- Adds to timeline at correct date
- Tags as "Sedgwick correspondence"
- Extracts claim numbers
- Notes who signed it

**Deadline Tracker:**

- Sees "20 days to submit forms"
- Calculates exact deadline
- Sets reminder alerts
- Flags if approaching/missed

**Contradiction Detector:**

- Compares return date to HR's version
- Flags: "4 different dates found"
- Documents the contradiction
- Marks as constructive discharge evidence

**Policy Violation Detector:**

- Checks Sedgwick's own procedures
- Finds: "Should notify 7 days before return"
- Violation: Conflicting dates violate clear notice requirement
- Tags violation type and severity

**Result:** One document analyzed four different ways automatically.
