## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Transform homework stress into achievement with Vibe-Tutor!**

Designed specifically for students aged 13-17 with ADHD and autism, Vibe-Tutor combines powerful AI tutoring with neurodivergent-friendly features to help you stay organized, focused, and motivated.

### 🎯 CORE FEATURES

**Homework Dashboard**
• Voice-to-text homework entry
• Smart due date tracking
• Visual progress tracking
• One-tap task completion
• Weekly progress charts

**AI Tutor (Powered by Gemini with OpenRouter fallback)**
• 24/7 homework help across all subjects
• Step-by-step explanations
• Adapts to your learning style
• ADHD-friendly bullet-point responses
• No judgment, patient support

**AI Buddy**
• Emotional support companion
• Uses your interests (like Roblox!) to explain concepts
• Helps with motivation and focus
• Safe, monitored conversations

### 🧠 NEURODIVERGENT SUPPORT

**Sensory Settings**
• Adjustable animation speeds (none/reduced/normal)
• Sound controls
• Haptic feedback toggle
• OpenDyslexic font option
• Multiple color modes (default/high-contrast/warm/cool)
• Customizable font sizes

**Focus Timer (Pomodoro)**
• 25-minute focused study sessions
• Earn points for every minute
• Prevents screen sleep during sessions
• Visual countdown progress
• Achievement system

**Visual Schedules**
• Morning and evening routines
• Step-by-step task breakdowns
• Timer for each step
• Completion tracking
• Earn tokens for finished routines

**First-Then System**
• Structured reward system
• Complete routine steps to unlock games
• Parent-configurable requirements
• Teaches self-regulation

**Brain Games**
• Word Search with difficulty levels
• Earn stars and tokens
• Hints available (adjust challenge)
• Perfect for study breaks
• Subject-based worksheets

**Token Economy**
• Earn tokens for tasks and routines
• Roblox-style reward system
• Track earnings and spending
• Transparency for parents and students

**Achievement System**
• Unlock badges for milestones
• Progress tracking
• Earn bonus points
• Celebrate successes
