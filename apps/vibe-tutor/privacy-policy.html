Vibe Tutor — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

Vibe Tutor is a personalized AI-powered learning assistant designed for students ages 13 and older, including neurodivergent learners (ADHD, Autism Spectrum, and ODD support). We take user privacy seriously.

Data We Collect

  • Chat Messages: Conversations with the AI tutor are processed in real-time to generate educational responses. Messages are not stored permanently on our servers — they are only held in memory during an active session.
  • Session Tokens: A temporary, anonymous session token is generated each time the app connects. It does not identify the user. Tokens expire after 24 hours.
  • Analytics Events: Lightweight, pseudonymous usage events (for example, feature usage counts) may be collected to improve reliability and learning experience quality. No personally identifiable information (PII) is required.
  • Voice Input Transcripts: If you use voice homework entry, microphone audio is converted to text on-device by platform speech services and the resulting transcript is sent to our AI processing flow to structure assignment details.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or account credentials.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect photos, videos, or device files.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We only share the minimum required data with AI processors (Google Gemini and OpenRouter) when you use AI chat features.

AI Processing

Chat messages are sent to Google Gemini (primary) or OpenRouter (fallback) to generate educational responses. We do not permanently store chat content on our backend. Third-party AI providers may process and retain request data according to their own policies.

Voice homework transcripts use the same processing path as text inputs and are subject to the same third-party AI provider processing terms.

Permissions We Request

  • Microphone (optional): Used only when you tap voice input for homework entry. You can deny this permission and continue using typed input.
  • Media and storage access (optional): Used for music download/playback and local export features when enabled by the user.

Content Safety

All AI responses pass through content filtering to ensure child-appropriate content. Inappropriate language, violence, and adult content are automatically blocked.

Data Storage

  • On-device: Learning progress, preferences, and token balances are stored locally on the device using browser storage. This data never leaves the device.
  • Server-side: No personal data is persisted on our servers. Sessions are ephemeral and expire automatically. Limited pseudonymous operational logs may be retained for reliability and troubleshooting.
  • Diagnostics: Crash and runtime diagnostics may be collected in release builds to improve stability. Diagnostics are limited to technical telemetry and are not used to identify users by name.

Age Scope

Vibe Tutor is intended for users ages 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information, contact us and we will investigate and remove it when applicable.

  • No account creation is required.
  • No direct collection of name, email, or phone number is required to use the app.
  • Parent settings are protected by a PIN.

Data Deletion

You can delete local app data from Settings → Data Management → Clear All Data. This clears locally stored progress and settings on the device.

We do not keep long-term user profiles on our backend for deletion requests.

Security

All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Rate limiting and content filtering protect against misuse.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
